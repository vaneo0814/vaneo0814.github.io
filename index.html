A mans best-friend

2 reasons why dogs are the best pets

  • They make sure no food ever goes to waste- If you're full up from dinner but can’t stand the thought of tipping any leftovers into the rubbish, your furry little dustbin is always on hand to help.

  • They make you active – even when you really, really can’t be bothered to go for a stroll, the necessity of ‘walkies’ means you've just got to get out there. And you always feel glad that you’ve had the exercise.
